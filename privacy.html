Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-01

Introduction

Mindoku is a Sudoku game for iOS designed to deliver a clean, focused puzzle experience. Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding that data.

Information We Collect

  • Anonymous device data: such as OS version and device model.
  • Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA): used to show personalized advertisements where available.
  • Anonymous analytics events: gameplay statistics and crash reports to help improve stability and features.

We do not directly collect personally identifiable information such as your name or email address within the app.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate core features of Mindoku:

  • Google AdMob (advertising). View their Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat (in-app purchases and subscriptions). View their Privacy Policy.

How We Use the Data

  • To display relevant advertisements and measure ad performance.
  • To analyze usage and improve gameplay, features, and stability.
  • To support purchases and restore access to paid content.

Data Sharing & Security

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • Access to data is limited to the developer and necessary services.
  • Reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards are applied to protect data.

User Choices

You may opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your iOS settings:

Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and disable app tracking/Limit Ad Tracking.

Data Retention & Deletion

  • We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined above.
  • If you wish to request data deletion, contact us using the email below.

Children’s Privacy

Mindoku is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will always be available on this page, and the “Last updated” date will reflect the latest changes.

Contact Us
